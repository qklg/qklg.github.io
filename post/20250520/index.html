2 注册失败

https://hetzner.cloud 打开注册
注册之前把你的梯子关了，用你中国的ip直接注册hz
邮箱我用的outlook，卡的话我绑定的招商银行的visa，
地址的话用我身份证上的地址用谷歌直接英文翻译，名字的话用的英文
然后手机打开https://accounts.hetzner.com/ 注册完之后会左边有个Vertification
但是我没遇到可以用身份证的，拍照上传护照，旁边那张纸写上你的hz的Client number，名字的英文签名，

和护照身份信息页，就护照上有照片，出生年月，姓名啥的那一页，那张纸拍照的时候一起拍照上传

但是上传完，我就遇到了破事，直接失败了

3 邮件对线

这时候不要急，写邮件对线客服了
用你注册hz的邮箱
收件人填写如下 support@hetzner.com accountverification@hetzner.com

主题

Request for Assistance with Account Registration

正文模板如下

Dear Hetzner Support,
I have already provided my passport during the registration process, but I still received a message stating that you have decided to deactivate my account due to some concerns regarding the information.
Could you please help me understand why this happened? If you require any additional identification or documentation, I am more than willing to provide it.
I sincerely hope you can assist me with this issue.
Thank you very much.
Best regards
你的实名名字拼音

然后hz客服收到的话，会给你回一个邮件的，

前面会有个提示自动回复的，不要管，

客服人工回的里面会有个链接，用手机打开，记得把梯子关了，然后浏览器会要摄像头权限的，给一下权限

我是选择的password护照实名，先要页面护照对护照身份信息页，就护照上有照片，出生年月，姓名啥的那一页

然后就是人脸识别了，识别完大概需要个把分钟吧，就显示ok了

再给客服回邮件，模板如下

Dear Hetzner Support Team,
Thank you for your help. I have completed the verification using my Chinese passport and facial recognition through the link you provided. Could you please confirm whether I have successfully passed the verification and if I am now able to purchase Hetzner's services? 
Thank you.

3 注册成功

4 吃二十欧

先买台机子，我反正开了台最便宜的arm，在我探针上

开完之后的话，去https://console.hetzner.cloud/support

选择account，other issue

把我下面的贴上去，aff链接我用的群友的

https://hetzner.cloud/?ref=tKuopBZRSSxG.

这个是群友的aff，群友教我了一堆，

我基本上注册遇到的问题都是群友帮忙的，所以我下面的怎么吃aff用的他的链接，真的感谢他

你觉得不合适的话可以换了

Dear Hetzner Support Team,

I recently registered for a Hetzner Cloud account using the referral link https://hetzner.cloud/?ref=tKuopBZRSSxG. My account was successfully created, and I have just purchased an ARM-based cloud server.

However, I noticed that the €20 referral signup credit does not appear in my account under https://console.hetzner.cloud/usage.

Could you please kindly check and let me know if it’s possible to apply the €20 referral credit to my account balance?

Thank you very much for your assistance.

Best regards,
你的名字

然后今天的话，成功到账

打开 https://console.hetzner.cloud/usage

可以看到了

整个hz的注册大概花了4天吧
